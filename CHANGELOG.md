## [0.1.0] - 2024-04-26
### Added
- Initial release of KRALN: Into the Dark
- 6 magic staves: Fire, Frost, Lightning, Shadow, Poison, Bone
- 6 enemy types: Ghosts, Skeletons, Demons, Bats, Wraiths, Zombies
- 5 levels: Graveyard, Crypt, Abyss, Lich Keep, Inferno
- Boss fights with multi-phase rage modes
- Status effects: Burn, Freeze, Poison, Lifesteal, Chain Lightning
- Combat system: Dash, Shield, Parry, Double Jump
- Particle effects: trails, explosions, ambient fog
- Bilingual support: English and Portuguese
- GitHub Pages deployment
